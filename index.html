Beliefs Inventory Quiz

Take the quiz to uncover particular irrational ideas which contribute to unhappiness and stress based on the work of Albert Ellis. Albert Ellis developed a system to attack irrational ideas or beliefs and replace them with realistic statements about the world through a system he called Rational Emotive Therapy. This assessment of your irrational beliefs is the first step to identifying unrealistic self-talk.
